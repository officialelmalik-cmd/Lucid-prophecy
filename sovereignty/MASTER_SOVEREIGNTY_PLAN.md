### Federal

**R&D Tax Credit (Section 41)**
- Value: 20% of qualifying R&D expenses
- NEOSAI qualifies: AI development, platform engineering, algorithm development
- Retroactive: Can amend prior 3 years of returns
- Payroll Tax Offset: As a startup, can offset up to $500K/year in payroll taxes
- Action: Hire R&D tax credit specialist (contingency fee basis available)

**WOTC (Work Opportunity Tax Credit)**
- Value: Up to $9,600 per veteran hired
- Applies to your first hires at NEOSAI
- File IRS Form 8850 within 28 days of each hire

**Section 179 Deduction**
- Deduct full cost of equipment/software in year purchased
- Applies to all NEOSAI tech infrastructure

**QBI Deduction (Qualified Business Income)**
- 20% deduction on pass-through business income
- Applies if structured as LLC/S-Corp

### California State

**California R&D Tax Credit**
- 15% of qualifying research expenses (24% for amounts over prior year)
- Stacks with federal R&D credit

**New Employment Credit**
- Up to $56,000 per qualifying new hire in designated areas
- Check if Moreno Valley qualifies
