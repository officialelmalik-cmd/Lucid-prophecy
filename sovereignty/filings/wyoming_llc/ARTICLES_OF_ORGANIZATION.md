## IMMEDIATE NEXT STEPS AFTER FORMATION

1. **EIN Application** → https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
   - Takes 10 minutes, immediate
   - Free

2. **S-Corp Election** → IRS Form 2553
   - File within 75 days of LLC formation
   - Saves 15-30% in self-employment taxes at scale

3. **Business Bank Account**
   - Mercury Bank: https://mercury.com (best for startups)
   - Need: EIN + Articles of Organization

4. **California Foreign Registration**
   - URL: https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov ($70)
   - Required to do business in California

5. **SAM.gov Registration**
   - URL: https://sam.gov
   - Required for ALL federal contracts
   - Use your Wyoming EIN
