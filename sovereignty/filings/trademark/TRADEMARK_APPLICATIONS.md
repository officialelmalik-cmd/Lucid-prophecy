## STEP-BY-STEP FILING PROCESS

1. Go to: https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/apply
2. Click "TEAS Plus" (cheapest)
3. Create/login to USPTO account
4. Search mark first: https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search
   (Make sure it's not already taken)
5. Fill application:
   - Mark text
   - International class(es)
   - Description of goods/services
   - Dates of first use
   - Specimen (screenshot showing mark in use)
6. Pay filing fee
7. Submit

**Timeline:** 8-12 months for registration
**Immediate protection:** From filing date

## WHAT TO SEARCH FIRST (Pre-filing clearance)

Go to: https://tmsearch.uspto.gov

Search for each mark before filing. If similar marks exist in your class,
you may need to modify the mark or consult a trademark attorney.
