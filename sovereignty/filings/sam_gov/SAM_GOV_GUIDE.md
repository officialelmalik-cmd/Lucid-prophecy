# SAM.GOV REGISTRATION GUIDE
## The Foundation — Required for ALL Federal Opportunities
## URL: https://sam.gov | Free | 7-10 days to activate

---

## WHY THIS IS FIRST

Without SAM.gov registration you CANNOT:
- Apply for federal contracts
- Apply for SBIR/STTR grants
- Receive most federal grants
- Get certified as SDVOSB, 8(a), HUBZone

This is the master key. Do it first.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP REGISTRATION

**Step 1: Get EIN**
- URL: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- Takes 10 minutes, immediate
- Free

**Step 2: Get D-U-N-S / UEI Number**
- SAM.gov now uses UEI (Unique Entity ID) — generated automatically
- Old DUNS no longer required

**Step 3: Go to SAM.gov**
- URL: https://sam.gov
- Click "Sign In" → "Create an Account"
- Use Login.gov for authentication

**Step 4: Start Entity Registration**
- Click "Register New Entity"
- Select "Business or Organization"

**Step 5: Enter Core Data**
- Legal business name
- Address
- EIN
- Business type

**Step 6: Enter Financial Information**
- Banking info for electronic payments
- This is how you get paid on contracts

**Step 7: Enter Points of Contact**
- Government Business POC (you)
- Electronic Business POC (you)
- Past Performance POC (you)

**Step 8: NAICS Codes**
- Enter all codes listed above
- Mark 541511 as primary

**Step 9: Representations & Certifications**
- Small Business: YES
- SDVOSB: YES
- Minority-Owned: YES
- Answer all questions honestly

**Step 10: Submit**
- Review takes 7-10 business days
- You'll get email confirmation when active

## AFTER SAM.GOV IS ACTIVE

**Immediately do:**
1. Apply for SDVOSB at: https://veterans.certify.sba.gov
2. Apply for SBA 8(a) at: https://certify.sba.gov
3. Register at: https://www.dodsbirsttr.mil (DoD SBIR portal)
4. Register at: https://sbir.nasa.gov (NASA SBIR portal)
5. Register at: https://beta.sam.gov (search contract opportunities)

**Set up alerts:**
- Go to beta.SAM.gov → "Opportunities"
- Filter by NAICS codes + SDVOSB set-aside
- Save search and enable email alerts
